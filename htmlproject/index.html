Hygeia's Gift

Brand name

Our brand name is Hygeia's Gift, which represents a gift from the goddess of health. As part of our mission statement, we aim to provide amazing gifts for our loved ones on different occasions that are beneficial to their health. We use Hygeia's Gift as our brand name to indicate that fruits are the giving of nature and giving for our health! We want everyone to constantly consume foods to power up their day as fruits provide energy to our bodies. Through our brand, we also want to radiate positive energy to our customers by selling organic fruits.

Logo

In our brand logo, we incorporated fruits and leaf circles to demonstrate our mission. The fruits represent the gifts of the goddess and the leaf circle represents that the fruits are also nature-giving. We wanted to make the logo simple but memorable and meaningful to our customers at the same time.

Mission statement

We strive to supply organic fruits as precious gifts for our unique ones during special occasions, such as birthdays, valentines, and the new year. Hygeia's Gift aims to provide everyone with the most beneficial and organic fruits to promote ways of maintaining everyone's health. We want our people to live long and healthy.

About our fruit basket

Our fruits are 100% organic. On the top left corner, there is a "Fruit" switch table that displays all the fruits we offer for the fruit basket. There is another "Order" switch table for you to order a fruit basket. Depending on the occasion and the quantity amount of the fruits, the size and the decoration of the basket would vary. Every basket looks different, but the basket is the shape listed in the "Order" switch tab. The quantity of each fruit will vary based on the fruits you have chosen, we will provide the right amount that produces a gorgeous fruit basket.
